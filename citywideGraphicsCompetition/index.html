Unity has nothing but benefits. It has proven its worth to many people. When alone, it is more likely for life to beat you down and what-not; this is when we recognize the potential of unity. It helps us create a family of people to look up to and assist in any way possible. This helps us through difficulties and makes us accomplish things we never could have done by ourselves.
